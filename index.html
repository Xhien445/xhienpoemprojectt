Echoes of Elara

In the land where waters whisper tales,
Elara wanders, her spirit pales.

Once a beacon, bright and clear,
Now shadowed by the weight of fear.

Her heart, a sea of endless sorrow,
Drowning dreams of bright tomorrow.

In fountains' depths, she seeks reprieve,
Yet finds only what hearts deceive.

Beneath the surface, a storm does brew,
A soul adrift, with pain so true.
But hope, a flicker, still resides,
In the depths where sorrow hides.

In the mirror of the moonlit sea,
Elara glimpses what she can be.

With every tear and every sigh,
She learns to spread her wings and fly.
